面试准备之开放性问题

Posted on February 13, 2013 in 面试 面试

1 Self introduction

Hi,my name is Shuai Zhao,a graduate student from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, majored in Computer Science and Engineering. In the next, I’ll introduce myself from two aspects.

On the major side, in my undergraduate, I spent most of my time in ACM/ICPC(international colleagues programming contest). It’s a algorithm contest and has a high requirement on thinking ability and coding ability. Due to interest, I learned the things needed on the internet and was able to qualify in the onsite contest in Hangzhou and Peking and finally I got two bronze medals.

In 2009 to 2010, I developed the interactive electronic board with three other teammates. This project is meaned to take the place of blackboard in the classroom and whiteboard in the conference room. It combines the features of Photoshop and Powerpoint. Nowadays, it has been sold in the northeast and southwest of China and two of my teammates have founded the funmi technology based on this application.

As I’m very interested in machine learning, I finished the stanford machine learning online course with programming exercise 795/800.In the summer of 2012, I participate in the 21st machine learning summer school in Kyoto University.

On the personality side, I think I have a very good teamwork spirite. I’m very interested in football. I have been playing football since 1997. In this game, I learned the true meaning of teammate. Only in a team, could one do a bigger thing than by oneself. I play football for at least three hours per week. It’s real interesting to encourage the teammates and handle the difficulties in the match. I gain a lot from football. What’s more, I think I have a good communication ability. I acted as a monitor in my undergraduate class for one and a half years and can communicate well with the classmates which have very different backgrounds.

2 Why Japan

Firstly, I like challenge. In a new environment, I’m forced to learn new things, meet new friends, think more and do more. Secondly, I think it’s a good place to concerntrate on the advanced techniques in Japanese companies, as I have been to Kyoto University this summer holiday, I think it is the right place. At last, Japan has lots of playgrounds to play football, that is a big plus.

3 Why LUXEYS

Firstly, programming and football are my hobbies. I think it’s the right place to combine them in LUXEYS.Secondly, I think LUXEYS has a very bright future. In China, there’s a sport post bar in China called zhibo8 which is very popular among the young. The PV per day is over 1 million. This post bar is only founded in 2011. I think LUXEYS can do better.

4 advantages and benefits

I have strong programming skill, good learning ability, good teamwork spirit and true love to football. So I can spent no efforts on the work, because it’s not only my work but also my two hobbies.

5 evaluations on me

targeted,work hard and enjoy challenges.

6 plan in the next five years

In the first about five years, I want to concentrate on the techniques. As I think only in a team may one make a big difference, I want to lead a team and mainly focuse mainly on the industry level.

7 working interest

I want to do something on football SNS. I want to provide better statistic on the match and more convenient way for the users to communicate with each other. When LUXEYS has enought users, data mining techniques especially recommencation system is necessary, I want to do something on that area.

8 Project introduction

I want to introduct the interative electronic board. It’s intented to take the place of blackboard in the classroom and whiteboard in the conference room. It’s a combination of Photoshop and Powerpoint as it has multi objects on multilayers. Each Object can be reedited and layers can be merged. What’s more, it has other professional tools such as intelligence pen and compasses. Two of my teammates have founded the fangmi Tech based on this software and it has been sold in northeastern and south western part of China.

9 research achievement

My paper A Fast Spatial Partition Method in Bounding Volume Hierarchy has been accepted by IEEE International Conference on Software Engineering and Service Sciences, the related patent has been accepted as well. The method is to improve the efficiency of real time ray tracing. The building time is 39% of the latest method SBVH.

10 programming language

C/C++(>150,000 lines), C#(>20,000 lines),Matlab,Python,Java has finished related project as well.

11 application

I have developed one website on Google application engine which is meant to help my girl friend to remember sth important and predict the next event day and sent email ahead of time.
